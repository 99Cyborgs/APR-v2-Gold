# Evidence Anchors

Evidence anchors are structured references with two fields:

- `location`
- `quote`

APR v2 emits anchors for parsing findings, reviewability decisions, scientific-record criteria, integrity concerns, and pack outputs. Anchors keep reasons attached to visible text surfaces instead of free-floating prose. Renderers may summarize anchors, but they may not replace or mutate the anchor payload in the canonical record.
